The Best Movies According to Geetha

My top 5 movies of all-time.

A Man Called Otto

This is a simple happy-go-lucky movie that you can watch with family

Peanut Butter Falcon

This is a beautiful movie about a determined boy who is undettered by his physical health.

Cast Away

This is a great movie about a guy who is thrown into a challenging situation.

Bahubali

A great masterpiece delivered by a South Indian director

Shawshank Redemption

One of the best movies that will keep you on the edge of your seat throughout.
